For our project, we chose to analyze suicide in the world using the Suicide Rates Overview 1985 to 2016 dataset. It was created by a Kaggle user who combined datasets (joining by time and place) from the United Nations Development Program, the World Bank, and the World Health Organization. The pulled-in data provides additional information about each country, including each country’s human development index and GDP statistics. Additionally, the dataset organizes suicide occurrences by year, sex, and age.

What country’s suicide rate decreased the most since 1987?

# get the average suicide rate in each country
suicide %>%
  filter(age == "5-14 years") %>%
  group_by(country) %>%
  summarise(avg = sum(suicides_no) / sum(population)) %>%
  arrange(desc(avg))
